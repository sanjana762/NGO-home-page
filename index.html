About Helping Hands

Helping Hands is a non-profit organization founded in 2010 with the belief that every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and access to essential resources. We strive to uplift marginalized communities by focusing on education, healthcare, women empowerment, and child welfare.

Over the years, our grassroots initiatives have reached over 25,000+ lives across urban slums and rural villages in India. Through volunteer-driven campaigns, free medical camps, digital literacy drives, and food distribution programs, we’ve created long-lasting social impact.

At Helping Hands, we strongly believe that real change begins at the community level. Our approach combines empathy, data-driven planning, and consistent fieldwork. We’re powered by passionate volunteers, local partners, and everyday citizens like you.

Join us in building a future where no one is left behind. Together, we can create a world of equal opportunities and lasting compassion.
